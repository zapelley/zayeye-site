The gap this company sits in.

Most AI consultants know the technology but have never run a submittal log. Most construction-tech vendors sell a product, not a fix for how your work actually flows. Big consultancies are generic and expensive. Zay Eye Solutions exists in the gap: credible on the jobsite, capable with the technology.

The founder

A civil engineer with hands-on, contractor-side design-build delivery experience in water and wastewater — the person who has sat in the coordination meetings, chased the RFI, and assembled the owner report from four systems at 6 p.m. And, separately earned: the ability to design, build, and deploy real AI systems, not just recommend them.

That combination is the whole pitch. When we map your workflow, you don't have to explain what a submittal is. When we build the tool, nobody else is subcontracted to build it.

How we work

  • Grounded, not hype. We say what a thing does, what it's worth, and what it costs — then stop.
  • Human-in-the-loop, always. AI output is decision support your people review and own. Never an autonomous decision. This is structural in how we build, not a policy promise.
  • You own the asset. No metered black box. The system, the data structures, and your encoded standards belong to your firm, and the AI inside is swappable.
  • We run on our own method. This company is organized and operated with the same system we sell. We eat the cooking.

The first step is a conversation, not a contract.
